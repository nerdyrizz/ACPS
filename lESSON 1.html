The most valuable resource

Water is the essence of life, essential for survival, sustaining ecosystems, supporting agriculture, and driving industries.

How to save water?

  • Turn off taps while brushing teeth or washing dishes to avoid unnecessary water wastage.
  • Fix leaking taps and pipes to prevent water loss.
  • Water plants early in the morning or late evening to reduce evaporation.
  • Adopt water-saving habits like taking shorter showers and using only what is needed.
  • Educate others about saving water .
